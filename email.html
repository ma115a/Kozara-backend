• Arrival Information

From Gradiška, follow the Kozara region road and resort signage. Remain on paved roads until the final section.

The last approximately 1 km is an unpaved natural road — completely normal and accessible with standard vehicles.

• Self-Catering

Kozara Panoramic Resort is a private self-catering retreat. We do not provide fresh food products before arrival for hygiene reasons.

  • Small shop: Turjak village (on your way)
  • Large supermarkets: Gradiška & Banja Luka
• Organic Garden

Seasonal organic baskets from our garden can be arranged upon request.

• Outdoor Cooking

Open fires and charcoal BBQs are not permitted. A modern electric smoker BBQ is available upon request.
